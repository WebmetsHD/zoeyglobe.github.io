[1] Sam (5 minute short story)
posted by Zoey [Thu Jan 25th, 2018]

I wrote this in about 5 minutes around the middle of December 2017 when I was in a horribly depressed mood about a week before I left my college. I was considering handing it in for my English class homework, but I didn't want them to inform my parents.
Don't worry, I'm much happier now than I was when I wrote this.



The sound of breaking glass within earshot of her bedroom door rarely had Sam worried, her heavily-inebriated father was breaking things all the time in a drunken haze- and it's not like it would be the first time Sam had cut herself on her father's bottle-remains, well, if she cut herself by accident it would be a first.

But hey~ let's not be thinking about that tonight, right?- can't have anybody visiting me in my time of need, can I?

Think of nice things, nice.. pretty things.., ♫ everything is fiine~♫, not tonight, fucking damn it!  

Hearing voices again are we, Samuel?  
"that's not my name..."  

You know, there's only one place that little boys like you go with voices in their head, Samuel.  
"whatever you're thinking, it's better than here.."
warm blood trickles from a self-created wound, running down the twitching arm and onto the cold ground.



Do you not know that you are God's temple and that God's Spirit dwells in you? If anyone destroys God's temple, God will destroy him. For God's temple is holy, and you are that temple. - 1 Corinthians 3:17



I was more thinking of a cemetery, but yet again, based on your current behaviour I'd say that a cemetery is more likely than whatever you were envisioning.

Oh: Samuel?- daddy's coming.
